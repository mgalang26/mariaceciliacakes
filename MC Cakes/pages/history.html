OUR History

We were established in 2013 as an online business making customized cakes. The costumer will claim their order thru pickup or delivery. After that the Maria Cecila Cakes was born. It started as a hobby and then into a business. June 28, 2019 we opened a physical shop after SM Savemore in San Vicente, Apalit, Pampanga, Philippines. As the shop was established, we launched variety of new products like Milk Tea that became the trend starting those years and Ice cream by Granny's for us to complete as a one stop shop for celebration.

Since then, we are committed in making quality homemade premium cakes for a full costumer satisfaction experience.
